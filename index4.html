    Facts About My School

    Started initially as Step by Step Nursery in 1996, it was only a matter of time that its reputation and organic growth would lead to the conceptualisation of what is known as Caelum High #The Happy School! Caelum started functioning at its brand new sprawling campus at Undri, Pune in 2014, with a total of 162 students.

    Through outstanding ICSE results coupled with holistic development of all our students, the good word spread and today we can proudly call ourselves one of the top ICSE institutions in the country. Our strength today is close to 1000 students and that number only increases every year.. The word Caelum pronounced as Kellum is a Latin word which means a chisel-shaped constellation of stars. An apt name for our institution, where we believe that every child is a star with a light of its own waiting to be discovered. Our dedicated teachers are synonymous to the chisel and ensure that they bring out the very best in every child.
